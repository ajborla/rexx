# Resources
The Rexx language was first conceived in 1979 with the mainframe as its target platform. Therefore, many of the listed resources are mainframe-centric, and may appear somewhat dated. Nevertheless, they are a rich source of Rexx knowledge, but should be read with care, and judgement exercised.
